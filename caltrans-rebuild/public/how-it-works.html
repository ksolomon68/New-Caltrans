How CaltransBizConnect Works

A streamlined platform connecting Small Business Enterprises with contracting opportunities

For Vendors & Small Business Enterprises

1

Create Your Account

Register as a vendor on CaltransBizConnect. You'll need basic business information including your business name, EIN, and contact details.

Create Account
2

Complete Your Profile

Add your business details, select your work categories and districts, and upload your capability statement (PDF, max 10MB).

Tip: A complete profile increases your visibility to agencies.

3

Browse Opportunities

Search and filter opportunities by district, category, and posting date. Each opportunity includes detailed project information, requirements, and contact details.

View Opportunities
4

Apply & Connect

Contact the agency or prime contractor directly using the information provided in the opportunity listing. Follow their specific application process.

For Agencies & Prime Contractors

1

Register Your Organization

Create an agency account with your organization details and authorized representative information.

Create Account
2

Post an Opportunity

Fill out the structured opportunity form with all required information including project description, timeline, budget range, and specific requirements.

Quality Standards: All postings must meet our quality requirements to ensure vendors receive complete, accurate information.
3

Search for Qualified Vendors

Browse vendor profiles, filter by category and district, and review capability statements to find qualified Small Business Enterprises.

4

Manage Your Postings

Track your active opportunities, update information as needed, and manage responses through your dashboard.
