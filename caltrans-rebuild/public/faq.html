Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about CaltransBizConnect

General Questions

You can download our template from the Resources page. Capability statements must be uploaded as PDF files with a maximum size of 10MB.

For Vendors & Small Business Enterprises

Opportunities are posted regularly as agencies have new projects. We recommend checking the platform frequently or enabling email notifications for new opportunities in your selected categories.

Opportunities that meet our quality standards are typically posted within 1-2 business days after submission for review.

Yes, you can edit your posted opportunities through your agency dashboard. Updates will be reflected immediately on the platform.

Use the vendor search feature in your dashboard to filter by work category, district, and certification status. You can review vendor profiles and capability statements to find qualified partners.

For Agencies & Prime Contractors

Caltrans districts, other government agencies, and prime contractors working on Caltrans projects can post opportunities on the platform.

You'll need to provide project title, description, location (district), work category, timeline, budget range, requirements, and contact information. All fields are required to ensure vendors receive complete information.

Opportunities that meet our quality standards are typically posted within 1-2 business days after submission for review.

Yes, you can edit your posted opportunities through your agency dashboard. Updates will be reflected immediately on the platform.

Use the vendor search feature in your dashboard to filter by work category, district, and certification status. You can review vendor profiles and capability statements to find qualified partners.

All postings must include complete project information, clear requirements, realistic timelines, and accurate contact details. This ensures vendors can make informed decisions about applying.
