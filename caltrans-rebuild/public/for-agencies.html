For Agencies & Prime Contractors

Connect with qualified Small Business Enterprises, post structured opportunities, and streamline your contracting process through our quality-focused platform.

Create Agency Account Learn More

Why Use CaltransBizConnect?

Quality Assurance

Our platform enforces quality standards for all opportunity postings, ensuring complete information and reducing back-and-forth communication.

Find Qualified Partners

Access a database of certified Small Business Enterprises with verified capability statements, certifications, and past performance records.

Efficient Management

Manage all your opportunity postings from a single dashboard. Track submissions, extend deadlines, and communicate with vendors efficiently.

How It Works for Agencies

  1. Create Your Agency Account: Register with your organization details and authorized representative information.
  2. Post an Opportunity: Complete our structured form with all required fields, including scope, district, work category, and due date.
  3. Administrator Review: Your posting enters a review queue and will be published within 2 business days after approval.
  4. Manage Postings: Track active opportunities, view submissions, and communicate with interested vendors.
  5. Automatic Archival: Expired opportunities are automatically archived after their due date, keeping your listings current.

Opportunity Posting Requirements

Quality Standards: All opportunity postings must include complete information in the following required fields. Incomplete postings cannot be submitted and will not be published.

Project Title

Clear, descriptive title for the opportunity

Scope Summary

Minimum 100 characters describing the work

District/Region

Caltrans district where work will be performed

Work Category

Type of work (roadway, bridge, electrical, etc.)

Due Date & Time

Submission deadline (must be a future date)

Submission Method

How vendors should submit proposals

Contact Information

Point of contact for questions

Contract Specifications

Detailed requirements and specifications

Review and Approval Process

To maintain platform quality and ensure all postings meet Caltrans standards, we have implemented a review workflow:

  • All opportunity postings are reviewed by an administrator before publication
  • Review turnaround time is 2 business days
  • You will receive notification when your posting is approved or if revisions are needed
  • Once approved, your opportunity will be visible to all registered vendors
