Support Services

Training, technical assistance, and business development programs to help you succeed

Small Business Enterprise Supportive Services

The SBE Supportive Services Business Development Program (SBE/BDP) provides comprehensive support to help small businesses compete for and successfully complete Caltrans contracts. Our services are designed to strengthen your business capabilities and increase your competitiveness.

Technical Assistance

Get expert guidance on various aspects of contracting and business operations:

  • Estimating & Bidding: Learn how to accurately estimate project costs and prepare competitive bids
  • Contract Management: Understand contract requirements, compliance, and best practices
  • Project Planning: Develop effective project schedules and resource allocation strategies
  • Quality Control: Implement quality assurance processes and documentation
  • Safety Programs: Develop and maintain comprehensive safety programs
Request Assistance

Training Programs

Participate in workshops and training sessions covering:

  • Business Fundamentals: Accounting, financial management, and business planning
  • Marketing & Networking: How to market your services and build relationships
  • Proposal Writing: Creating compelling proposals that win contracts
  • Technology & Innovation: Leveraging technology to improve efficiency
  • Compliance & Regulations: Understanding regulatory requirements
View Upcoming Training

Business Development

Grow your business with our development services:

  • One-on-One Consulting: Personalized business counseling and strategic planning
  • Capability Statement Review: Professional review and feedback on your capability statement
  • Bonding Assistance: Guidance on obtaining bonding and insurance
  • Financial Planning: Cash flow management and financial forecasting
  • Growth Strategy: Planning for business expansion and diversification
Schedule Consultation

Networking Opportunities

Connect with agencies, prime contractors, and other small businesses:

  • Matchmaking Events: Meet agencies and primes looking for subcontractors
  • Industry Forums: Participate in discussions about industry trends and opportunities
  • Vendor Fairs: Showcase your capabilities to potential clients
  • Peer Networks: Connect with other small business owners for support and collaboration
View Events

Certification Support

Get help with the SBE certification process:

  • Eligibility Assessment: Determine if your business qualifies for SBE certification
  • Application Assistance: Help completing the certification application
  • Document Preparation: Guidance on gathering and organizing required documents
  • Renewal Support: Assistance with certification renewal process
Learn About Certification
