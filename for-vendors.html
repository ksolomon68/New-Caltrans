How CaltransBizConnect Helps You Succeed

Find Opportunities

Search and filter contracting opportunities across all Caltrans districts. Receive notifications for opportunities matching your expertise and service areas.

Showcase Your Capabilities

Upload your capability statement and profile to demonstrate your core competencies, past performance, and qualifications to agencies seeking partners.

Streamlined Process

Our platform guides you through every step, from account creation to opportunity application, eliminating confusion and decision fatigue.

Your Journey to Success

  1. Create Your Account: Register as a Small Business Enterprise with your business information and contact details.
  2. Complete Your Profile: Add your business details, certifications, and service areas to help agencies find you.
  3. Upload Capability Statement: Showcase your core competencies, past performance, key personnel, and qualifications.
  4. Select Districts and Categories: Choose the Caltrans districts and work categories that match your expertise.
  5. Search and Apply: Browse opportunities, filter by your preferences, and submit proposals directly through the platform.

What You Need to Get Started

Required Information:
  • Business name and legal structure
  • Federal Tax ID (EIN)
  • Business address and contact information
  • SBE certification number (if applicable)
  • Capability statement (PDF format, maximum 10MB)

Capability Statement Requirements

Your capability statement must include the following information to help agencies evaluate your qualifications:

Core Competencies

Detailed description of your primary services and areas of expertise

Past Performance

Examples of completed projects with scope, value, and outcomes

Key Personnel

Qualifications and experience of your leadership team

Equipment & Facilities

Resources available to complete contract work

Financial Capacity

Bonding capacity and financial stability indicators

Certifications

Current certifications, licenses, and insurance coverage
